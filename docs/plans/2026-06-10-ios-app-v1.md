## Deviations

- Reviewer-driven: AI entry chooser is a confirmationDialog, not a sheet
  (sheet-identity swap mid-presentation is fragile).
- StyleTransfer sheet title is "Style transfer" (sketch said "Image input"
  before the D2 split).
- Operational: never restart CoreSimulatorService to "fix" registration --
  it resets the runtime image registry.
